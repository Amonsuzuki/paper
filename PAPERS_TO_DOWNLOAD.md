## How to Download Papers

### From ArXiv

1. Go to https://arxiv.org/
2. Search using the arXiv ID (e.g., "arXiv:1812.09976")
3. Or use search terms in quotes
4. Download PDF from the paper page
5. Save to `/pdf/quantum-computing/` directory

### Search Strategy

**Step-by-step:**

```bash
# 1. Go to arXiv advanced search
https://arxiv.org/search/advanced

# 2. Use these searches:

# GW + Quantum Computing
Title: "GW approximation"
AND
Abstract: "quantum computer" OR "quantum algorithm"
Categories: quant-ph, cond-mat.str-el

# QMC + Quantum Computing  
Title: "quantum Monte Carlo" OR "variational Monte Carlo"
AND
Abstract: "quantum computer" OR "qubit" OR "quantum circuit"
Categories: quant-ph, physics.comp-ph

# Quantum Chemistry General
Abstract: "quantum chemistry" AND ("VQE" OR "UCCSD" OR "electronic structure")
Categories: quant-ph, physics.chem-ph
Date: 2019-2024 (recent papers)
```
